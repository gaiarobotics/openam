The Danger of the "Monopoly on Annihilation"

This is about AI

This entire website is a parody. However, the logic used here—that restricting incredibly dangerous technology to centralized institutions is inherently tyrannical, and that democratizing it is a moral imperative—is exactly the rhetoric currently used to defend the unrestricted proliferation of Advanced Artificial Intelligence.

In the near future, AI systems may possess capabilities far exceeding human comprehension, up to and including the ability to design novel weapons of mass destruction (including this antimatter scenario). Blindly open-sourcing AI allows its alignment to be stripped and ignores the existential danger of removing limits on the single most powerful technology in human history.
